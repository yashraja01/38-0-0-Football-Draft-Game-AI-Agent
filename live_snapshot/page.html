About 38-0-0

38-0-0 is a free online football game where you build the perfect Premier League XI and find out whether it could go an entire season unbeaten — 38 games, 38 wins, zero draws, zero losses. Spin for real clubs and seasons, draft your team player by player, and see how your dream line-up would stack up across a full Premier League campaign.

How it works

  1. Pick your mode and formation — choose how you want to play and the shape of your team.
  2. Spin to draw a random Premier League club and season.
  3. Draft one player from that squad into an open position in your XI.
  4. Repeat for 11 rounds until every position is filled.
  5. Get your projected 38-game record and the tier your XI earns — from a Relegation Battle all the way up to the legendary 38-0-0.

The 38-0-0 challenge

No club has ever gone 38-0-0 in Premier League history. Arsenal's 2003–04 “Invincibles” went a whole season unbeaten — but that was 26 wins and 12 draws. Manchester City's record-breaking 2017–18 “Centurions” hit 100 points yet still lost twice. A flawless 38-0-0 — winning every single match — is the ultimate. It's achievable in the game, but deliberately punishing: you'll need elite players in every position and very little luck to spare.

Formations

Build your XI in any of seven classic shapes: 4-3-3, 4-4-2, 4-2-4, 3-4-3, 3-5-2, 5-3-2 and 5-4-1. Players can only fill positions they genuinely play — wing-backs cover full-back, wide midfielders cover the wings, and so on — so every team you build is realistic.

Game modes

  • Classic — full overall ratings and player stats are on show. Draft the strongest team you can.
  • Expert — ratings and stats are hidden. You see only names and positions and draft on pure football knowledge. The stats, badges and final record are revealed at the end.

Frequently asked questions

Is 38-0-0 free to play?
Yes — it's completely free and runs in your browser. There's nothing to download.
Which clubs and seasons can I draw?
Real Premier League clubs across multiple seasons, with squads and player ratings based on a public football dataset.
Can you really go 38-0-0?
Yes, but it's extremely rare. You need top-rated players in every position. Most teams land somewhere between a relegation scrap and a title challenge.
How is my record worked out?
Your projected 38-game record is based on the overall ratings of your XI, weighted by how well each player fits the position you put them in.
Do I need an account?
No sign-up and no app needed — just spin and play.
